The MotoYatra Journey

What started as a small passion project in 2025 has grown into one of the most trusted motorcycle rental experiences in the region. MotoYatra was created by riders who wanted to share the joy, freedom, and thrill of the open road with everyone.

Through dedication, constant improvement, and a love for motorbikes, our fleet expanded, our services improved, and our community grew stronger. Every bike we prepare is checked with care, ensuring every rider enjoys a safe and memorable adventure.
